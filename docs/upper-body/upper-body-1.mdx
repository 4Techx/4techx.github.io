# Upper Body Workout Plan (No Equipment)

### Exercise 1: Arm Circles
<ImageComponent imageUrl={EXERCISE_IMAGES.arm.circle} alt="Upper Body" />
#### Beginner Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 2 sets of 15 repetitions (forward and backward)
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 5 minutes
- **Notes**: Use small and controlled movements.


#### Professional Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 20 repetitions (forward and backward)
- **Rest**: 20 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 7 minutes
- **Notes**: Focus on keeping your arms parallel to the ground.

---

### Exercise 2: Push-Ups
<ImageComponent imageUrl={EXERCISE_IMAGES.pushup.basic2} alt="Upper Body"/>
#### Beginner Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 2 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Rest**: 40 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 5 minutes
- **Notes**: Perform with knees on the ground for better control.

#### Professional Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 4 sets of 12-15 reps
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 8 minutes
- **Notes**: Maintain a straight body and engage your core for stability.

---

### Exercise 3: Tricep Dips (Using a stable surface, if available, or perform on the floor with bent knees)
<ImageComponent imageUrl={EXERCISE_IMAGES.tricep.dips} alt="Upper Body" />
#### Beginner Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 2 sets of 6-8 reps
- **Rest**: 45 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 5 minutes
- **Notes**: Keep your knees bent for ease and focus on slow movement.

#### Professional Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 4 sets of 12-15 reps
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 8 minutes
- **Notes**: Straighten your legs to increase difficulty.

---

### Exercise 4: Plank
<ImageComponent imageUrl={EXERCISE_IMAGES.plank.basic} alt="Upper Body" />
#### Beginner Level
- **Duration**: 3 sets of 20 seconds
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between sets
- **Total Time**: Approximately 5 minutes
- **Notes**: Place your knees on the ground if needed.

#### Professional Level
- **Duration**: 3 sets of 45 seconds
- **Rest**: 20 seconds between sets
- **Total Time**: Approximately 6 minutes
- **Notes**: Engage your core and ensure your back stays straight.

---

**Warm-Up**: Arm circles (Exercise 1) naturally act as the warm-up.  
**Cool-Down**: 5 minutes of stretching or yoga poses.
