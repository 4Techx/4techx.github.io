# 🏋️‍♂️ Upper Body Workout Plan

## **Day 1: Chest & Triceps**
- 🏋️‍♂️ **Bench Press** - 3 sets of 8 reps  
- 💪 **Push-ups** - 3 sets of 15 reps  
- 🔄 **Triceps Dips** - 3 sets of 12 reps  

## **Day 2: Back & Biceps**
- 🏋️‍♂️ **Pull-ups** - 3 sets of 10 reps  
- 💪 **Bicep Curls** - 3 sets of 12 reps  
- 🔄 **Rows** - 3 sets of 8 reps  

## **Day 3: Shoulders & Core**
- 🏋️‍♂️ **Shoulder Press** - 3 sets of 10 reps  
- 💪 **Plank Holds** - 3 sets of 30 seconds  
- 🔄 **Russian Twists** - 3 sets of 20 reps  

_Repeat or mix these exercises throughout the week for a complete upper-body routine! 🚀_
