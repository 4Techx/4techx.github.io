# Upper Body Mixed-Level Bodyweight Workout Plan

## Equipment Needed
- None (bodyweight only)

---

### Workout Plan

1. **Wall Push-Ups (Beginner) / Standard Push-Ups (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 12 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 15 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~5 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~20 calories  
   - **Focus**: Chest, Triceps  

2. **Knee Push-Ups (Beginner) / Diamond Push-Ups (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 10 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 12 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~6 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~25 calories  
   - **Focus**: Chest, Triceps  

3. **Modified Plank (Beginner) / Full Plank-to-Shoulder Tap (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 15 seconds hold (Beginner) / 4 sets of 10 taps per side (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~7 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~30 calories  
   - **Focus**: Core, Shoulders  

4. **Incline Push-Ups (Beginner) / Decline Push-Ups (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 8 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 10 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~8 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~35 calories  
   - **Focus**: Chest, Shoulders  

5. **Superman Arm Extensions (Beginner) / Superman with Arm and Leg Lift (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 10 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 12 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~8 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~35 calories  
   - **Focus**: Back, Shoulders  

6. **Modified Dips using a Low Surface (Beginner) / Full Bodyweight Dips (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 8 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 10 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~10 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~40 calories  
   - **Focus**: Triceps, Chest  

---

### Total Workout Time: ~44 minutes  
### Total Calories Burned: ~185 calories
