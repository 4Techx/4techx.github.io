# Upper Body Workout Plan (With Equipment)

### Exercise 1: Dumbbell Front Raise (Light)
<ImageComponent imageUrl={upperBodyImageUrl1} alt="Upper Body" />
#### Beginner Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 2 sets of 12-15 reps
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 5 minutes
- **Notes**: Use light dumbbells (2-5 kg) and focus on controlled, steady movements.

#### Professional Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 4 sets of 12-15 reps
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 8 minutes
- **Notes**: Use moderate weight dumbbells (5-8 kg) and maintain proper form.

---

### Exercise 2: Dumbbell Lateral Raise (Light to Moderate)
<ImageComponent imageUrl={upperBodyImageUrl1} alt="Upper Body" />
#### Beginner Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 2 sets of 10-12 reps
- **Rest**: 40 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 6 minutes
- **Notes**: Use light dumbbells (2-4 kg) and keep your arms slightly bent.

#### Professional Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 10 minutes
- **Notes**: Use moderate dumbbells (5-7 kg) and control the movement.

---

### Exercise 3: Dumbbell Bicep Curl (Moderate)
<ImageComponent imageUrl={upperBodyImageUrl1} alt="Upper Body" />
#### Beginner Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 2 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Rest**: 45 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 5 minutes
- **Notes**: Use light to moderate dumbbells (3-5 kg) and avoid swinging your arms.

#### Professional Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 8 minutes
- **Notes**: Use moderate to heavy dumbbells (6-8 kg) for a greater challenge.

---

### Exercise 4: Bench Press (Heavy)
<ImageComponent imageUrl={upperBodyImageUrl1} alt="Upper Body" />
#### Beginner Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 2 sets of 6-8 reps
- **Rest**: 1 minute between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 6 minutes
- **Notes**: Use a light to moderate barbell or dumbbells and ensure proper technique.

#### Professional Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Rest**: 1.5 minutes between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 12 minutes
- **Notes**: Use a heavier barbell or dumbbells while maintaining controlled movement.

---

### Exercise 5: Pull-Ups (Body Weight)
<ImageComponent imageUrl={upperBodyImageUrl1} alt="Upper Body" />
#### Beginner Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 2 sets of 3-5 reps (use assistance if needed)
- **Rest**: 1 minute between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 5 minutes
- **Notes**: Focus on controlled motion, especially lowering down.

#### Professional Level
- **Sets/Reps**: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Rest**: 90 seconds between sets
- **Duration**: Approximately 10 minutes
- **Notes**: Perform strict pull-ups with a full range of motion.

---

**Warm-Up**: Start with 5 minutes of dynamic stretches or light cardio.  
**Cool-Down**: Finish with 5 minutes of stretching or yoga poses to relax and recover.
