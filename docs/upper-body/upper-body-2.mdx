# Upper Body Mixed-Level Dumbbell Workout Plan

## Equipment Needed
- Dumbbells of varying weights

---

### Workout Plan

1. **Dumbbell Front Raise (Beginner) / Dumbbell Lateral Raise (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 10 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 12 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~6 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~20 calories  
   - **Focus**: Shoulders  

2. **Dumbbell Bicep Curl (Beginner) / Alternating Dumbbell Bicep Curl (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 12 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 10 reps per arm (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~7 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~25 calories  
   - **Focus**: Biceps  

3. **Dumbbell Chest Press on Floor (Beginner) / Dumbbell Chest Press on Bench (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 12 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 10 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~8 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~30 calories  
   - **Focus**: Chest  

4. **Dumbbell One-Arm Row (Beginner) / Dumbbell Bent-Over Row (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 10 reps per arm (Beginner) / 4 sets of 12 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~8 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~35 calories  
   - **Focus**: Back  

5. **Dumbbell Shoulder Press (Beginner) / Dumbbell Arnold Press (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 12 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 10 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~10 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~40 calories  
   - **Focus**: Shoulders  

6. **Dumbbell Push-Up with Rows (Beginner) / Renegade Rows (Professional)**  
   - **Sets/Reps**: 3 sets of 10 reps (Beginner) / 4 sets of 12 reps (Professional)  
   - **Time**: ~10 minutes  
   - **Calories Burned**: ~45 calories  
   - **Focus**: Chest, Back, Core  

---

### Total Workout Time: ~49 minutes  
### Total Calories Burned: ~195 calories
